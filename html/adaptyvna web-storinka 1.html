The Walk

The wslk from Monterosso to Riomaggiore will take you approximately two hours, give or take on hour depending on the weather conditions and your physical shape.

What?

Cinque Terre compires five villages: Monterosso, Vernazza, Corniglia, Manarola, and Riomaggiore.

Where?

On the northwest cost of the Italian Riviera, north of the city La Specia

Price?

The walk is free!
